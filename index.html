About Me

Photography for me is about stillness within motion — finding the decisive moment where light, subject, and geometry align into something that outlasts the shutter click. Based in Dubai, I photograph everything from the golden hour over the desert to the quiet geometry of urban life.

What started as a personal passion has become a sustained practice — always learning, always looking for the frame within the frame.
